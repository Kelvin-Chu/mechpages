What Is MechPages?

{#

#} {# Get personal with your mechanic.

#}

Free classified listing for finding local mechanics. The goal is to connect you directly with a local mechanic that you can trust so you don't have to worry about overpaying for repairs.

  • Browse

    Enter your location and Mechpages will show you the closest mechanics along with their work history, skill set, customer reviews, and more.

  • Request

    No mechanics found in your area? Post a service request with a description of your issue for a future Mechpages mechanic to see.

  • Reviews

    The reviews at Mechpages are restricted to users with a verified mobile number (excluding VOIP) to limit spam and paid reviews.

  • Message

    Send a message to as many mechanic as you want until you find the right one. Your email will be masked to protect your privacy.

Sign Up As A Mechanics

Be your own boss and earn more with Mechpages. There are no fees to set up an account with Mechpages and users will be able to find you as soon as you complete your profile. Standard features will always be free.

Free: Classified Listing

Once you have created a mechanic's account and completed your profile, you will appear in our classified listing.

Free: Browse requests

Head towards your toolbox and you will be able to browse through all the service requests that has been made by folks in your area.

In Development: Lawful History Badge

Earn the trust of your clients by signing up for a background check. We will not perform a background check unless it is authorized by you.

In Development: Work History Badge

Further gain the trust of your clients by signing up for a work history check. This check will ensure the clients that you have the experience you claim you do.

In Development: Featured Status

Earn good reviews and be eligible for the Featured Status where you will be listed first for searches in your area.

In Development: Electronic Receipt

Appear professional by using electronic receipt. All you have to do is list the repair that was done and the cost. We will send a copy to your client via email and store it for your reference later.

Why MechPages?

I absolutely loath going to an auto shop to get things done on my car, regardless of whether it is something big or small. It makes me cringe. On the other hand, honest mechanics are getting swindled by shop owners with poor wages and long hours. My goal is to provide an alternative to this crooked industry by connecting folks with a mechanic directly. I want my money to go 100% to the person working on my car, not to a needless middle man.
